How to Order a Commission

  1. Read the terms of service.
  2. Donate $25 USD or greater to a fundraiser supporting the people of Palestine.
    • Many fundraisers are in EUR or other currency. Regardless of currency, your donated amount must equal or exceed $25 USD when converted back.
    • Recommended fundraisers are listed below.
  3. Save your receipt email as a PDF.
    • Tutorial is linked here (from wayneradiotv).
    • Note that this receipt will likely have your full name. All sensitive information will be kept private and your receipt will be deleted once your commission is complete.
  4. Fill out the commission form.
    • I will be in contact with you when I get your submission to confirm your request!

I hope this method isn't too confusing for people! This way your money is going directly to the organizations/people who need it and we don't have to waste cents on transaction fees with me acting as a middle-man.

Terms of Service

Last Updated: 4/15/2024

1. General Conditions

By purchasing a commission you agree to these terms.

Commissions are for personal use only (profile pics, fan art, OC illustrations, etc).

Commissions are digital; nothing is intended to be physically printed or shipped.

2. Payment

A donation amounting to $25 USD or greater post-conversion buys you a bust commission. You are only sending me your receipt; the money never goes through me.

3. Commission Process

Turnaround time for commissions is expected to be a few weeks but will vary based on complexity and external factors (health, work schedule). I will be in touch if your work is expected to take longer.
