Conquest

CONQUEST (fka TIDES OF CONQUEST) is a 17th century pirate-adjacent historical fantasy based on westerns and yakuza films.

a small found family crew named BLACKTALON is employed by a criminal syndicate to smuggle religious texts out of the country. through this work, they find themselves navigating the politics of the underground in the middle of a gang war.
